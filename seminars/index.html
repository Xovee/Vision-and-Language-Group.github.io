Speaker: Qike Zhao [PPT (password: pjlk)]
Topic:Image Manipulation by Text
Date:May 29, 2020
Abstract: The task aims to semantically edit parts of an image according to the given text by a user, while preserving other contents that are not described in text. The key of the work is to exploit both text and image cross-modality information, generating the new attributes matching the sentence, and produce the high-quility images, even in the complex scene. In this report, I will briefly introduce the task, then introduce the progresses and ideas via some representative works. Two parts could be included in my report:
(1) A brief overview about image manipulation by text.
(2) Introduce a new work detailedly, then share the two relevant works simply.

Speaker: Zhibin Zhang [PPT (password: 5227) ]
Topic:Training Image Retrieval with a Listwise Loss
Date:May 29, 2020
Abstract: Image retrieval task usually uses pairwise or tuplewise loss functions which also called local loss functions.But such local loss functions often requires a number of ad hoc heuristics and only optimize an upper bound on the true ranking loss.There are two papers use listwise loss functions in Image retrieval tasks. Two parts are included in my report:
(1) A novel listwise ranking loss directly optimizing the mAP.
(2) A ranking-motivated structured loss(RLL) to learn discriminative embeddings.
