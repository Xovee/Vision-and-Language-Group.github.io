Speaker: Sitong Su [PPT (password: cyj1)]
Topic:Image Generation From Layout
Date:May 8, 2020
Abstract: Recent years have witnessed a wide variety of image generation works conditioned on diverse inputs, allowing our creative and imaginative nature flourishing. Among them, layout-to-image allows users to provide the layout of salient objects only(i.e. bounding boxes and corresponding category labels), and lets the model generate realistic images consistent with the specified layout. The core challenge is that how to generate fine-grained details and realistic textures without segmentation map input.
The report aims to help listeners have a generative understanding of the task and catch with the latest research trend. Two parts are included in my report:
(1) An simple overview about layout-to-image
(2) Introduce three papers in this area

Speaker: Qilong Zhang [PPT (password: vqyn) ]
Topic:Adversarial attack and defense
Date:May 8, 2020
Abstract: By adding human-imperceptible noise to clean images, the resultant adversarial examples can fool other unknown models. This raises security concerns about those machine learning algorithms. In order to understand DNNs better and improve its robustness to avoid future risks, it is necessary to investigate the defense models, and meanwhile the generation of adversarial examples. In order to give you a more comprehensive introduction. Today, my report includes three parts:
(1) An simple overview about adversarial attack
(2) Introduce some defense strategies
(3) Share the papers I submitted previously
