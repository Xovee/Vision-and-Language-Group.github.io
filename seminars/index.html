Speaker:Sitong Su [PPT&Video(password: 1234)]
Topic:Extensions of SPADE
Date:August 14, 2020
Abstract: Spatially Adaptive Normalization has made a huge progress in promoting the quality of semantically image synthesis. Moreover, the profound achievements of SPADE inspire researchers to probe into the way of injecting features in image synthesis which leads to the emerging publications on the extensions. These extensions could be summarized in three ways: 1. Applying the transformed SPADE on other tasks such as image translation, image manipulation. 2. Modifying SPADE to make it more versatile. In this report, we will introduce the extensions of SPADE and the latest progress in image synthesis.

Speaker:Haoyu Wang [PPT&Video(password: 1234) ]
Topic:Video-Text Matching and Captioning
Date:August 14, 2020
Abstract: For vision and language tasks, both visual-text matching and captioning tasks have a gap between vision and language. The two tasks have many similarities, the one task can help the other one to achieve good performance, and they even can use same datasets. I will introduce a paper about video-text matching which using hierarchical graph reasoning, it may help captioning task.
